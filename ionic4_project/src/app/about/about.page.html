The application

This is an unpublished university project by a small team of students.
The main purpose of the mobile application is to provide the means to view 24 hours and last hour crypto coin market.
Also you can save favorites and add coins in your portfolio.

Cryptocurrencies

A cryptocurrency is a digital medium of exchange using strong cryptography to secure financial transactions, control the creation of additional units, and verify the transfer of assets. Source: Coin Market Cap, What are cryptocurrencies
